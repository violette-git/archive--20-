Welcome to Dig Deep!

Getting Started

Dig Deep is an incremental clicker game where you dig deeper and deeper into the earth, collecting resources and discovering secrets along the way.

Click the DIG! button to start digging and collecting resources.

Resources

As you dig, you'll collect different resources:

  • Dirt - The most common resource, found near the surface
  • Stone - Found deeper underground, more valuable than dirt
  • Gems - Rare and valuable, found in the deepest layers

Use these resources to purchase upgrades that will help you dig faster and deeper!

Upgrades

Spend your resources on upgrades to improve your digging efficiency:

  • Tools - Improve your manual digging power
  • Automation - Dig automatically without clicking
  • Special - Unique upgrades with powerful effects

New upgrades will become available as you reach deeper depths!

Hazards

As you dig deeper, you'll encounter hazards that can slow your progress:

  • Cave-ins, gas leaks, and other dangers will appear
  • You'll get a warning before hazards activate
  • Spend resources to prevent hazards before they cause damage

Be prepared and keep an eye out for warning signs!

Discoveries

The deeper you dig, the more secrets you'll uncover:

  • Special discoveries at certain depths
  • Random events that can help or hinder your progress
  • Achievements to unlock for special bonuses

How deep can you go? What mysteries await in the depths?

Saving Your Progress

Your game is automatically saved every minute, but you can also:

  • Save manually using the Save button
  • Export your save file to back it up
  • Import a save file to restore your progress

Now you're ready to start digging! Good luck!
